# HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends. The dashboard helps HR teams and management understand key factors contributing to attrition and make data-driven decisions.

## Key Metrics
- Total Employees: 1470
- Total Attrition: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.1%
- Average Age: 37
- Average Salary: 6.5K
- Average Years at Company: 7

- Employees with Life Sciences and Medical education show higher attrition.
- Most attrition occurs in the lower salary slab (up to 5k).
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives have the highest attrition by job role.
- Early years at the company show higher employee turnover.

## Analysis Covered
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Gender
- Attrition by Education
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Attrition by Job Role

## Tools & Technologies

## Project Objective
To analyze HR data and identify patterns in employee attrition to support better hiring, retention, and workforce planning strategies.
